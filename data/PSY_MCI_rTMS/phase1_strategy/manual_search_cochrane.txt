How to use:
1. Go to https://www.cochranelibrary.com/advanced-search
2. Paste each #N line one at a time into the search bar and click 'Add'
   (the interface builds a numbered history list automatically)
3. Apply Year filter: Publication Year from 2000 to 2025
4. Run the final line (highest #N) and review results
5. Export: Export → Format: RIS → save as cochrane_search.ris
6. Place in: data/phase2_search/raw/cochrane_search.ris
7. Run: python scripts/run_phase2.py --deduplicate
