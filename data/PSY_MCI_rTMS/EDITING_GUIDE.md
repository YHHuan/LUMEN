## How to Edit

### Figures (forest plots, funnel plots, PRISMA, RoB)

Figures are generated by `src/utils/visualizations.py`. To customize:

1. **Quick re-label**: Edit `src/utils/visualizations.py` directly (search for axis labels, title strings, font sizes)
2. **Re-generate all figures**: `python scripts/run_phase5.py --builtin-only` (no LLM cost, just recalculates + re-plots)
3. **PRISMA diagram**: `python scripts/export_prisma_diagram.py` (can override counts with `--included N`)
4. **Write your own script**: Read `statistical_results.json` and use matplotlib/seaborn directly

### Manuscript Text

- Edit `.md` files in `phase6_manuscript/drafts/` directly
- To re-generate a single section: `python scripts/run_phase6.py --section discussion`
- To re-generate multiple: `python scripts/run_phase6.py --sections introduction,methods`
- Each re-generation costs ~$0.15–0.20

### Statistical Numbers

- All in `phase5_analysis/statistical_results.json`
- Structure: `{outcome_key: {k, studies, fixed_effect:{...}, random_effects_dl:{pooled_effect, se, ci_lower, ci_upper, p_value, heterogeneity:{Q, I2, tau2, ...}}}}`
- Re-run: `python scripts/run_phase5.py --builtin-only` (free, no LLM)

## Tips

- **Cheapest edits**: Modify text/figures locally, no need to re-run pipeline
- **Re-run Phase 5 only**: If you fix Phase 4 data, just re-run Phase 5 ($0.30) and Phase 6 ($1.10)
- **Don't delete checkpoints** unless you want to re-extract from scratch ($14+)
